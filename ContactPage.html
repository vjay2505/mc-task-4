Our Team

Hey there! We're just a bunch of film-loving students who stumbled upon the idea of unraveling the magic behind the camera. Our team might not be seasoned pros, but we're a passionate group, each bringing our unique perspectives and eagerness to explore the intricacies of filmmaking. From writing articles to snapping behind-the-scenes pics, we're here to share our enthusiasm and learn along the way. So, join us on this student-led adventure into the captivating world of film crews!
